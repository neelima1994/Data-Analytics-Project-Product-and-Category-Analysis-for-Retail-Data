# Product & Category Analysis for Retail Data

## Project Overview
This data analytics project focuses on analyzing a retail company’s product catalog to generate insights on **pricing, reviews, stock management, and return policies**.  
The goal is to improve **business operations and customer satisfaction** through data-driven decision-making.

---

## Objectives
- Analyze product pricing and stock patterns
- Identify top-rated and high-performing products
- Understand the impact of return policies on revenue and ratings
- Provide actionable insights for inventory and pricing optimization

---

## Dataset Overview
- **Records:** 5,000  
- **Columns:** 15  
- **Product Categories:** 3  
- **Warehouses:** 3  
- **Duplicates:** None (based on Product ID)  
- **Missing Values:** None  

### Key Attributes
Price, Stock, Reviews, Supplier, Warehouse, Return Policy, Ratings

---

## Key Insights (SQL Analysis)
- Premium-priced products exist across categories, suitable for targeted marketing
- Electronics generate high revenue despite lower stock, indicating strong demand
- No strong correlation between price and customer ratings
- Products with **30-day return policy** show higher stock, ratings, and revenue
- Top-rated products in each category act as “hero products” for promotions
- Warehouse B stocks the highest number of flexible return policy products

---

## Key Visual Insights (Tableau)
- Home category has the highest average product rating
- Warehouse C shows the highest product turnover
- Products with low ratings but high reviews indicate engagement but dissatisfaction
- Flexible return policies (7-day and 30-day) drive higher revenue, especially in Warehouse C

---

## Business Impact
- Improves inventory planning for high-demand products
- Supports better pricing and discount strategies
- Enhances customer trust through optimized return policies
- Helps identify products for promotion and quality benchmarking

---

This project demonstrates the use of **SQL-based analysis and visual storytelling** to translate retail data into meaningful insights that support operational efficiency and customer satisfaction.
